Activities & Programs in Rumah Bakti Dato' Harun



The orphans in this orphanage home are doing three types of activity in this home. There are:

Daily activities

The orphans are doing their own daily activities such as having meals, sports, shower, praying, studying and sleep. All of these routine is being supervised by a warden to ensure that the orphans do their routine according to arranged time. This is action has to be done in order to nurturing these orphans to be discipline and obey.



Weekly Activity


1. Recite Yaasin and Tahlil (every Thursday, Friday night after Maghrib Prayer)
2. Religious Class (on Friday, Saturday and Sunday.)
3. Agricultural and landscape activities (held every Saturday 8.00am - 9.00am)
4. Computer Class and Library Activity (on Saturday 10.00 am - 1.00 pm)
5. Wedding invitations
6. Watch television (Astro Broadcast)
Monthly programs

Monthly programs has been arranged by the student affairs from the school administration. The activities are Cleaning Inspection to the dormitories, Community Services etc.
