The History of Rumah Bakti Dato' Harun

For the sake of a sense of responsibility and sympathy for those orphans , a group of activists community and senior officers of the State Government has successfully set up and register the " United Nations Educational Orphans Worldwide " by Registration Number ARS / Tues / 50/66 on August 16, 1966.


With the approval of this application , the Committee had been active to achieve the objectives of its establishment . For late Dato ' Haji Harun Idris and his wife Datin Salmah Y.Bhg Suleiman , the State Government gave a piece of land in Hulu Kelang ( HS.231 ) covering 4.8 acres, according to the document approval number PHT.G.2 / 139 / 75 . To determine this project can be implemented successfully Y.Bhg late Dato ' Haji Harun Idris was appointed as Chairman of the Organization.



The significance behind the Logo

As a recognition of Y.Bhg late Dato ' Haji Harun Idris, the members of the United Nations Educational Orphan Selangor has agreed to change the name of Rumah Bakti Hulu Kelang ( RBHK ) to Rumah Bakti Dato Harun ( RBDH ) along with the icons change Rumah Bakti also amended and approved amendments by the Registrar of Societies on 29 July 2011.
